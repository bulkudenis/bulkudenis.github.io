About Albania

Albania is a country located in Southeast Europe on the Balkan Peninsula. It is known for its natural beauty, rich history, and welcoming people.


The capital city is Tirana, a vibrant city filled with culture, modern architecture and entertainment.


Albania has a population of approximately 2.8 million people and offers a unique combination of mountains, lakes, rivers and coastline.


Interesting Facts

  • More than 450 km of coastline.
  • Home to two UNESCO World Heritage cities.
  • One of Europe's fastest growing tourist destinations.
  • Known for hospitality and traditional cuisine.
