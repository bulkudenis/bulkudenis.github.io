Why Choose Albania?

Albania is one of Europe's most beautiful destinations, offering crystal-clear beaches, ancient castles, mountain adventures and a rich cultural heritage.

From the Albanian Riviera to the Albanian Alps, visitors can enjoy unforgettable experiences throughout the country.

What Makes Albania Special?

Beautiful Beaches

Enjoy Ksamil, Dhërmi and Himara on the Ionian coast.

Historic Cities

Explore Berat and Gjirokastër, both UNESCO sites.

Mountain Adventures

Visit Theth and Valbona for breathtaking scenery.
