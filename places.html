Top Tourist Attractions

Ksamil

Famous for crystal-clear waters, white sand beaches and small islands located off the coast.

Berat

Known as the City of a Thousand Windows, Berat is one of Albania's most historic cities.

Theth

Located in the Albanian Alps, Theth offers spectacular mountain landscapes and hiking trails.

Gjirokastër

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stone houses and impressive castle.

Valbona Valley

One of the most beautiful national parks in Albania, perfect for hiking and nature lovers.

Butrint

An ancient archaeological site and UNESCO World Heritage treasure.
